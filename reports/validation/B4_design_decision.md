# B4 Design Decision

Decision: `design_B4_integrated_next`

Validation supports designing, but not yet implementing, a B4 integrated variant. The design direction should keep B3_final as the core and selectively integrate:

- B1-style source inventory and corpus coverage discipline where literature mapping benefits from explicit source accounting.
- B2-style provenance, evidence-burden, and benchmark-rigor patterns where audit tasks require tighter protocol and claim-boundary tracking.

Do not implement B4 until a separate design step is approved. B3_final should remain the validated module candidate in the meantime.
